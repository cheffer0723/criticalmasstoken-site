What it is

MASS is an experimental community token exploring how everyday people can coordinate and learn together using transparent on-chain tools. The base token is intentionally simple, while governance is designed to evolve gradually over time.

  • Built on Base
  • Simple token design, no hidden mechanics
  • Documentation-first approach
  • Long-term direction: structured community governance

What it is not

MASS is not a guaranteed investment, not a fundraising vehicle, and not a promise of future profits. It’s a public experiment in coordination and transparency.

  • No “get rich quick” messaging
  • No unrealistic roadmap claims
  • No paid influencer promotions

Governance approach

MASS is currently in a Founding Phase. During this phase, the founding steward coordinates documentation, infrastructure, and early decisions while governance processes are designed openly.

Over time, governance is intended to evolve toward a structured community model through clear proposals, transparent decision-making, and reduced centralized influence.

  • Founding Phase: stewardship + documentation
  • Later: community proposals and governance tooling
  • Long-term: regular participants guide decisions

Transparency log

Major changes and decisions are intended to be documented publicly as the project evolves. This includes website edits, documentation updates, and governance framework iterations.
